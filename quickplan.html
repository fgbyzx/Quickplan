PURPOSE

The purpose of utilizing our digital planner "QuickPlan" is to remove the complexities of planning and managing processes, allowing user's access to easily set and complete goals, log daily journal records, and monitoring progress overtime across multiple devices, including smartphones, and computers. Moreover, QuickPlan will provide the flexibility of having data synchronization, planner personalization, and features such as reminder and notification prompts to keep their planning experience personal and motivating.


CONTENT

MULTIMEDIA USED

The multimedia utilized will include text content and images such as pictures and Graphics Interchange Format (GIF) that may display static or animated content.

SOFTWARE/APPLICATION

The Software/Application to be used includes ReadyMag, WebFlow, and Squarespace. The software that proves to be the most efficient in applying functionalities and design will be decided after further testing.


POSSIBLE TWIST

This digital planner's possible twist showcases two categories: a period tracker for an accurate calendar method, and a pomodoro timer option that will help students and workers alike keep focused and motivated in achieving their goal and commitment.


DESIGN AND LAYOUT

The website itself would be designed as a book. It would be respond with turning pages when the user starts engaging with it. Log in/sign up part will be the first page then the digital planner on the next page. The website would be displaying a calendar that's in black and white default color. Once clicked, the screen would display a digital journal where you can customize colors according to your mood. Both the period tracker and pomodoro timer will have black and white default colors ready for color customization. We will have button logos and labels for easier navigation on the website.
